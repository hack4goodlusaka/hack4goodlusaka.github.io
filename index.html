Developers versus Climate Change

This September, leading up to the UN Climate Summit, the world's leading technologists, environmental organizations, NGOs, and governments are uniting in the world's biggest hackathon for Climate Change. The Geeklist #hack4good 0.6 takes place 12-14 September and will see over 3,000 passionate software engineers, designers, entrepreneurs and innovators unite online and in venues in 45+ cities around the world to build apps, products and technologies to solve climate and environment challenges facing the world, from climate awareness challenges to adaptation to action.
